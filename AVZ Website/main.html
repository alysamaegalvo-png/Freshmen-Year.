OUR SERVICES

General Construction Services

Complete construction services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. From ground-up construction to significant restorations, AVZ handles every aspect of construction with care and professionalism.

Project Management

End-to-end project management ensures that your construction project is finished on time, within budget, and to the highest standards. Our team ensures that every aspect, from beginning planning to final inspection, runs well.

Renovation and Remodeling

Creating places that match your changing needs, whether for home or commercial premises. Our staff specializes in everything from kitchen and bathroom remodels to full-scale building renovations.

Design and Build

We provide a full-service design and build solution, working with you from concept to construction. Our in-house architects and designers work together to bring your idea to life while assuring quality and consistency throughout.

Commercial Construction

Specialized commercial construction services include office buildings, retail businesses, restaurants, and more. We understand the special requirements of commercial projects and concentrate on creating useful, sustainable. and visually beautiful facilities.

Residential Construction

High-quality residential building services for new houses, multi- family projects, and bespoke constructions. Whether you're building your ideal house or making a real estate investment, we produce outcomes on time and under budget.

Site Development and Excavation

Expert excavation, grading, and land clearing services are provided to prepare the site for development, AVZ ensures that your site is appropriately prepared for safe, stable construction, adhering to all regulatory criteria.

Concrete Services

We provide concrete foundations, slabs, driveways, walkways, and other concrete-related services for residential and commercial projects. With each pour, our staff ensures long-term, sustainable results.

Demolition Services

The safe and effective destruction of existing structures. whether for site preparation or renovation. We perform residential, commercial, and industrial demolition with minimal inconvenience and waste.

Supply and Material Procurement

AVZ Construction and Supply provides a comprehensive range of building supplies, including structural components and finishing products. We supply quality, cost-effective products purchased from reputable vendors to ensure that your project is equipped.
